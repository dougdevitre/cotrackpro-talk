How it works

For where you are

The same calm, neutral record underneath — shaped to what you need from it today.

Parents

Stay on top of what matters without living in your calendar.

  • A heads-up text before an exchange or a court/school deadline.
  • Reply CONFIRM or SNOOZE to a reminder.
  • Text LOG to capture something the moment it happens.
  • For high-priority items, a short call in a familiar, steady voice.

Professionals

Attorneys, GALs, mediators, evaluators, and counselors.

  • Families keep their own timestamped record — less chasing.
  • Reminders are neutral and time-based; no case detail in any message.
  • Nothing here characterizes either parent — it describes events and times.
  • You work from the same record inside the CoTrackPro app.

In crisis · unsure

Help is never gated behind signing in.

  • Text RESOURCES or SAFE for support links.
  • Call the line for crisis resources and calm, anonymous help.
  • Safety resources come from vetted national sources, always.
  • Not sure where to start? The safety box above is a good first step.

Under the hood

How the line works

The same engine that powers the CoTrackPro app — shaped for your phone.

Workflow-driven

The assistant runs CoTrackPro's real workflows and scenarios — handoff disputes, school incidents, mediation prep, first-hearing readiness, protective-order steps — so the help you get matches what the app would do, not generic chat.

A calm, familiar voice

When the voice line opens, calls speak in a steady, recognizable voice (a named ElevenLabs voice), kept short and neutral. Texting is live today; the voice line is in progress.

Minimal data, by design

We correlate messages by an opaque key — we don't log your raw number or message body. Opt-ins are recorded as consent; STOP is honored across both texts and calls. Educational support — not legal or clinical advice.

Consent & privacy

Built to be safe to use even when things are tense

Court-neutral by design. Messages describe events and times. They never characterize the other parent, and never include private case detail — a reminder says “exchange at 5pm,” not the specifics.

You're in control. STOP opts you out instantly and we honor it across both texts and calls; START brings them back. Standard message & data rates may apply.
